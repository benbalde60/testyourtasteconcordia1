The chicken breast is a lean cut of meat taken from the pectoral muscle on the underside of the chicken. Each whole chicken contains one chicken breast with two halves, which are typically separated during the butchering process and sold as individual breasts. This versatile cut can be grilled, baked, roasted, fried, barbecued, and boiled in countless ways.
